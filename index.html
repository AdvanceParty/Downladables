Talking to the Machine

Originally published May, 2017.

Over the past few years we have witnessed a resurgence of interest in conversational experiences. From chatbots to voice assistants, companies of all sizes have been forced to reevaluate their customer engagement strategy and completely reimagine the way they interact with their consumers and other businesses.

Talking to the machine was published by Advance Party (or, as it was called a the time, Y2) in 2017 and contains a wealth of background information and contemporary insight into the nature of human-machine interactions.
